[Address to Lord Shanmukha
O Lord Shanmukha, please protect me. Form of Sa Ra Va Na Bha Va
( Six syllable mantra - Considered to be mystic symbol for Kaumaaraas). Prayed by Lord of demigods, Lord Vishnu, Lord Brahma, Kings and Lord Siva.
Revered at heart by those who gave up evils like attachment. Worshipped by demigods and sages. Son of Lord Siva. Destructor of three longing desires (to acquire land, wealth and women).
Worshipped by various rulers of the world. Cause of happiness among Brahmanaas. King of yogis (Self realized people). Cause for the destruction of mind (regarded as liberation) that by knowing the difference between One-ness and Multiplicity]
