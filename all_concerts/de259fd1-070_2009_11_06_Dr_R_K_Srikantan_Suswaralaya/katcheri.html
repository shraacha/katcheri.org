Upload Notes

2009_11_06_Dr_R_K_Srikantan_Suswaralaya

vocal : Vid Sri R K Srikantan
Vocal Support : Vid Rudrapatnam Ramakanth
Violin : Vid Charulatha Ramanujan
Mridanga : Vid H S Sudheendra
Ghata : Vid Ranganatha Chakravarthy



6th Nov 2009
Bangalore

Had sri RKS performed SRI raga, it would have been my first concert to listen to all Ghanaragas in the same concert

A very melodious concert by a 90 year old Artist. The energy during performance was abundant.
