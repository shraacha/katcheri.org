    Friends, I agree with all your sentiments. Please do pay close attention to the various srutibhedams during the alapanas of Thodi, Abheri and Kalyani. In Thodi, BMK brings out Shankarabharanam in Dha sruti and Kalyani in Ri. Next, you can hear Suruti in Ma sruti during the middle portion of the Abheri alapana. Finally, there are 2 different srutibhedams for the Kalyani RTP; BMK sings Mohanam in Ri sruti and MSG in his response plays Hindolam in Ma. These srutibhedams seamlessly flow throughout the alapanas. I hope these observations are easy to understand and to your satisfaction.
