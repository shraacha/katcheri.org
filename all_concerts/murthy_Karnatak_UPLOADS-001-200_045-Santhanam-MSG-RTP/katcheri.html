[Address to Lord Krishna- O Nandagopaala, Mukunda, who resides at the banks Yamunaa river, please protect me. You carried Mandara mountain as Kurmaavataar to churn the Milk ocean (during the fight between gods and demons). Player of beautiful flute. Destroyer of demon Madhu. Your face is attractive wearing beautiful smile. Your feet is aesthetic. Your eyes are like lotus flowers. Protect your sub-ordinates. Wear golden dress. Have five headed serpent as bed. Annihilator of evils of Kali yuga. Your heart is filled with compassion. Worshipped by group of sages. Your company is joy to Lord Shanmukha. Abode of Vaikunta. Form of Pure Consciousness and bliss. Uplifted Nanda govardhana. Joy to gopikaas]

kelo sachharita manuja kelo sachharita = hear good (sat) - o man always hear the sat

drida bhaktiyindali hariyanu nenedare koduva mokshave saakshi
drida bhaktiyillade hariya neneyadare koduva narakave saakshi - kelo
The moksha that is gained is the saakshi(evidence) of having prayed to Hari with bhakti
The naraka that is gained is the saakshi of not having prayed to Hari without bhakti

kanyadanava maadidavarige hennina bhogave saakshi= pleasure of woman is the saakshi for those who do kanyadaana
kanyadanava madadavarige hennina katave saakshi = Those who dont do kanyadana will be troubled by women (kaata=trouble)

pankti vanchana maadidavarige (na) rogave saakshi= Those who dont follow rules of pankti (eating order) get afflicted
pankti vanchana madadavarige putra bhagyave saakshi = Those who observe the rules are blessed with sons

anna danava madidavarige umbuva ootave saakshi = Those who do anna daana of food are blessed with food
anna danava madadavarige annakkarivare saakshi = Those who dont do anna daana should learn from hungry souls.

aadida maatige hariye saakshi ; = Hari is the saakshi for the spoken word
todida daarige neere saakshi = Water is the saakshi for the canal dug i.e. water flowing proves the suitability of a canal
maadida paapake manave saakshi; = Ones concience is the only saakshi for sins committed
saakshige purandara vittalana saakshi= evidence is evidence only because of purandara vittala
