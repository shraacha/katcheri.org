Venkatesha Kalyaanothsava concert
at the Hindu Temple Society, Augusta, Ga
on Jan 20, 2007.

Salem Shriram- Vocal
Jayashankar Balan-Violin
Ram Sriram-Mridanga.

1.Begade-Anudhinamulu
2.Aarabhi-Oni ulaga endhe

4.Kaamaach- Karpoora
5.Lathangi-Venkataramana
6.Shuddha Dhanyaasi-Bhaavamulona
7.Atthaana-Amba nee irangaadha
8.Hindola-Yaare Rangana yaare Krishnana
9.Kaambodhi- O'O' Rangashaayee

11.Aanadhabhairavi-Neeke theliyaka pothe
12.Behag- Naaneke badavanu

16.Sri-Nagumomu
17.Madhyamavathi-Mangala.
